This happy pastry is here to teach you how to host a live website.

Step 1: Mix the dough and shape it.

Welcome to step one! You will first create your files in a fancy text editor. In order to properly host a site be sure to have your main html file named index.html, this is important.

Step 2: Bake the dough.

In step two you will commit your code and push it neatly packaged over to Github. When your code is ready and the repository is all set, move on to the last step.

Step 3: Remove from the oven, cool, then enjoy your crossant.

In step three you will create a Netlify account, connect your GitHub and chose a repository to deploy! Give Netlify a moment to think and there you have it. Fresh out the oven. Share your creation with the world.
